# KeepCoolTogether

## Background

The world is warming and resources are limited, so together, let's find ways to keep cool, without breaking the climate bank. 

Objectives of _Keep Cool Together_ (KCT):
* Open source, low cost, low energy cooling systems
* Capture advice that helps people keep cool
* Nurture a creative community with solutions that are accessible to all
